Connecdo
Empowering the “I wish this existed” moments — where everyday ideas meet builders who bring them to life.

🚀 About Connecdo
Connecdo is an innovative platform designed to connect problem seekers with problem solvers. Users can share ideas or pain points that make them say, “I wish this existed,” and developers or builders can discover these ideas and create real solutions.

Connecdo aims to spark creativity, collaboration, and rapid innovation by turning everyday frustrations into tangible projects and products.

✨ Features
Idea Submission: Anyone can submit an idea or a problem that needs a solution.

Developer Marketplace: Builders can browse ideas and find projects to contribute to.

Karma Points: Devs have their own real identity, they get reputation and karma points based on solutions they provide.

Status Tracking: Keep users updated on the progress of their requested solutions.

Open & Inclusive: Encouraging community-driven innovation for real-world problems.

🎯 Why Connecdo?
We believe that great solutions come from identifying real needs. Too often, brilliant ideas go unheard or are hard to find by developers who want to build meaningful projects. Connecdo bridges this gap — empowering users to voice their needs and builders to answer the call.
